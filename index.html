Our Mission

Bringing hope
to the world

Go Serve Global is a faith-based non-profit dedicated to transforming communities across the globe. Through the love of Yeshua (Jesus Christ), we build schools, support pastoral homes, and bring lasting change to those who need it most.

01

Faith

Rooted in the love of Yeshua (Jesus Christ), our work is guided by an unwavering faith that moves mountains and transforms lives across every border.

02

Love

Every act of service flows from a heart of love. We believe that compassion without borders is the most powerful force for change.

03

Hope

Where there is darkness, we bring light. Through education, shelter, and community, we plant seeds of hope that bloom into futures full of promise.

Current Project

Building Hope in Kakamega, Kenya

In the heart of western Kenya, we are building a school and a pastoral home to serve the community of Kakamega. This project will provide education for children, training for local pastors, and a beacon of hope for the entire region.

Meet Our Founder

Leticia Bravo

30+
Years of Service
3+
Countries Served
∞
Lives Touched

Leticia lives in California and has dedicated her life to serving others, both as a missionary and as a Certified Nurse Assistant for over 30 years. Caring for people, whether through physical healing or spiritual support, has always been her calling.

Over the years, she has participated in numerous mission trips. She has supported the Hope Rehabilitation Center (Centro de Rehabilitación La Esperanza) in Tijuana, Mexico, providing essential items for men and women in recovery. She has also traveled to Cuba to bring gifts and basic necessities to children in need, and has contributed donations to churches wherever possible, striving to share love and hope at every turn.

Recently, due to an injury, Leticia has been unable to work, making it difficult to contribute financially to the mission work so close to her heart. Despite this setback, she still feels deeply called to continue serving, because the need has never been greater.

That calling led her to found Go Serve Global, a non-profit organization dedicated to bringing hope, healing, and resources to communities in need around the world.
